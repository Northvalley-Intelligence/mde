# Client Success CRM Context Pack

Applicable artifact profiles:

- `crm`
- `client_success`
- `support_workflow`
- `private_client_data`
- `client_facing_workflow`

Guardrails:

- Track client profile, projects, support requests, software requests, delivery history, confidentiality, recurring problems, and future software opportunities.
- Preserve enough context for future MDE missions without exposing private internal strategy to clients.
- Model support/request lifecycles explicitly.
- Record reusable support lessons to project outbox for central import.
